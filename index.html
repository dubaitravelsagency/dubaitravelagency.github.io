Morning Desert Safari- Adventure, sunrise view, refreshing breakfast, and more

Travelers who visit Dubai have one thing in common in their to-do list and that is surely a desert safari. If you are also planning your itinerary for Dubai and want to spend your day most fruitfully, then the Morning Desert Safari could be a great choice for you. Because, after the conclusion of the safari, you will have enough time to visit other tourist attractions in Dubai.

Complete safari details

  • Free cancellation: If you cancel your Morning Desert Safari tours a few days before the scheduled date, you will get a complete refund. If you cancel the tour in less than 24 hours before the tour, you will not get a refund for your bookings.
  • Book now and pay later: To provide you with flexibility in plans, we have included the option to book now and pay later.
  • Tour duration: If you have opted for the Morning desert safari, then it will take around 4-5 hours to complete.
  • Tour guide: An English-speaking tour guide will be provided to you during the duration of the tour.
  • Pick-up services: In our morning desert safari package, we also provide you with pick-up and transfer services. For pick-up, you can choose any hotel or apartment in locations such as Dubai, Ajman, and Sharjah. Pick-up options from Dubai Cruise Terminal are also available.

Morning Desert Safari

Dubai is certainly a dynamic city that serves as a great tourist attraction for individuals belonging to different walks of life. The morning desert safari tours come with a wide selection of activities that you can choose to take part in such as quad biking, bungee jumping, camel rides, falconry, and many more. Morning desert safari may not come with the BBQ meal option and activities that take place only during the night time, but it comes with a refreshing breakfast. During the start of the tour, your guide will pick you up from your location in a 4×4 vehicle. Then you will be served some welcome drinks following which you can enjoy clicking some good pictures with a beautiful desert view as the backdrop. At the desert site, you will be given the opportunity to bash the sand dunes, go sandboarding, or enjoy Arabic delights such as tea, coffee, rose water, and dates. Get your hands and feet painted with henna, wear traditional Arabic attire, and click your pictures in it.

Who should not go for the Morning Desert Safari?

  • Children under the age of 3 are not allowed on this safari
  • Pregnant women
  • If you have a back/heart problem, this safari is not recommended
  • Individuals in wheelchair

Things you need to carry with you

  • Wear closed-toe comfortable shoes
  • Carry a pair of sunglasses
  • For sun protection, wear a straw hat
  • Do not forget to apply sunscreen
  • Please wear clothing based on the weather
  • Do not carry any packaged items or large bags with you

Things you need to know

  • Quad bike is a self-driven activity and hence you will be responsible for any risks and safety hazards
  • Please buy international travel insurance beforehand
  • If you are aged between 9 to 15, an adult should accompany you
  • Quad biking is one of the Morning Desert safari activities that is not included in the package
  • Do not eat anything 2 to 3 hours before the start of your trip
  • In case of any changes in the departure time, you will be notified well in advance
  • During the holy month of Ramadan, some entertainment activities may not take place
